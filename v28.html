Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cyber Essentials?
Cyber Essentials is a UK government-backed scheme that helps organisations protect themselves against common cyber attacks. It provides a clear statement of the basic controls all organisations should implement to mitigate the risk from common internet-based threats.
How often should we conduct a Cyber 'MOT'?
We recommend conducting a Cyber 'MOT' at least annually. However, for organisations in high-risk industries or those experiencing rapid growth, more frequent assessments may be beneficial, such as quarterly. We provide discounted subscription pricing for ongoing engagements.
What does MS365 hardening involve?
MS365 hardening involves implementing security best practices to strengthen your Microsoft 365 environment. This includes configuring multi-factor authentication, setting up data loss prevention policies, enabling advanced threat protection, and more.
How long does a typical penetration test take?
The duration of a penetration test can vary depending on the scope and complexity of your systems. Typically, a standard penetration test can take anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks. We'll provide a more accurate timeline after assessing your specific needs.
How do you provide a 24/7 service?
V28 Cyber has offices in the UK and India which allows us to provide a round-the-clock service to our clients for ongoing monitoring engagements. All of our cybersecurity professionals are fully vetted, qualified and highly experienced in their field of expertise.
How do you price your services?
We understand pricing transparency is important and aim to provide a fixed fee up front for all of our services based on time spent and complexity of your infrastructure. Contact us for a bespoke quote.
